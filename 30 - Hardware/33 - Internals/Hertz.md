The measurement of cycles per second in a [[CPU|processor]] where 1 hertz (1Hz) is equal to one cycle per second.

| Title           | Speed            |
| --------------- | ---------------- |
| Hertz (Hz)      | 1cps             |
| Megahertz (MHz) | 1,000,000        |
| Gigahertz (GHz) | 1,000,000,000cps |

Modern processors can run at around 5GHz.
